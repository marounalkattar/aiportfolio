Introduction

Most people try their voice assistant for a few simple tasks—asking Siri the weather, or Google a random factoid—but don’t dive much deeper. Maybe it seems too complicated, you’re worried about privacy, so I build my own voice assistant from scratch using python.

How voice assistants work

Main loop:
1- Listen to a wake word (the name of the AI)
2- If heard listen for a known command
3- Execute the known command
4- Speak response
You can check the flowchart in the picture on the top

Some skills of my voice assistants:

At first, I named my voice assistant spiderman, so spiderman can tell me the current date, time, and weather. he Can introduce himself, can take input from CMD or from MIC. He can send emails and WhatsApp from WhatsApp web, search on Wikipedia, search on google, and play YouTube videos or music. He can give me the News update, read the text for me and give me Covid-19 update, open documents for me, tell me jokes, take a screenshot, record a reminder and a to-do list, give me information about the CPU and the battery and finally and the most important is that he can turn on a light on an Arduino or turn it off

Purpose of this project:

Building this voice assistant project can be used to upgrade my smart home project and to build a robot with AI.
